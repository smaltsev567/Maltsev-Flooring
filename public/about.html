About Maltsev Flooring

With over 20 years in the flooring and contracting business, Maltsev Flooring has grown from a small family workshop into Arlington’s go‑to flooring experts. Our foundation is built on trust, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

We believe in honest communication and transparent pricing—so you always know exactly what to expect. From selecting the perfect hardwood species to coordinating permits and inspections, we handle every detail so you can sit back and watch your vision come to life.

Our team of seasoned professionals brings expertise in solid wood, engineered, laminate, and vinyl plank installations, as well as flooring repair and refinishing. Whether you’re renovating a cozy bungalow or fitting out a large commercial space, we deliver durable, beautiful floors that stand the test of time.
