Services

General Contracting

From initial blueprint review to final walk‑through, our general contracting service handles all aspects of your build. We coordinate sub‑trades, secure permits, manage timelines, and ensure every phase meets strict safety and quality standards. Whether it’s a kitchen remodel, new addition, or full‑scale commercial fit‑out, we give you a single point of accountability and expert oversight.

Solid Wood Floors

Experience the warmth and character only genuine solid hardwood can deliver. We source top‑grade oak, maple, and walnut boards, acclimate them properly, then nail and finish each plank for a seamless, long‑lasting surface. Our multi‑coat finishing process seals in the grain, resisting everyday wear while enhancing natural color variations. With routine maintenance, your solid wood floors will remain heirloom quality for generations.

Engineered Wood Floors

Get the beauty of hardwood with added stability—engineered wood floors lock multiple veneers over a plywood core. This construction resists moisture, minimizes expansion/contraction, and installs easily over radiant‑heat systems or concrete. Choose from an array of hardwood species and finishes that look virtually indistinguishable from solid boards. Our precision installation guarantees tight seams and a perfectly level surface.

Laminate Floors

Laminate offers a budget‑friendly alternative without sacrificing style. We install high‑definition, scratch‑resistant laminate planks that mimic everything from distressed barnwood to travertine tile. The tongue‑and‑groove locking system clicks together for a floating floor that’s quick to install and easy to replace. Perfect for busy households, laminate stands up to traffic, pets, and kids while cleaning up in seconds.

Vinyl Plank Floors

Our luxury vinyl plank (LVP) flooring combines waterproof performance with authentic wood or stone looks. Each plank features a durable wear layer that withstands spills, heavy foot traffic, and even commercial use. With realistic embossing, bevels, and a variety of plank widths, you can achieve any aesthetic—from rustic farmhouse to sleek modern. Installation is fast: we glue, click‑lock, or float panels directly over most existing subfloors.

Floor Repairs & Refinishing

Give your aging floors a new lease on life. We tackle gouges, water stains, pet damage, and sun‑faded areas by matching filler, sanding, and re‑staining just the affected boards or entire rooms. Then we apply factory‑grade sealants and protective top‑coats to restore uniform appearance and durability. Our meticulous approach ensures repairs blend seamlessly so your guests never know anything was “fixed.”
